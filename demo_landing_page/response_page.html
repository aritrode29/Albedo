Hypothesis

The green building pre-compliance process is often manual and error-prone. CertiSense focuses on automated error detection, recommendations, and faster reporting to support LEED submissions.

Data & Sources

Uses LEED v4 rating systems documentation (BD+C, O+M, ID+C) and internal submission documents when available. Can pivot to building codes if submission data is not accessible.

Outputs

  • Error detection highlights
  • Credit recommendations and next steps
  • Audit trail and quick export for reviewers

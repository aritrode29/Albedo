What is Albedo and how does it work?

Albedo is an AI-powered assistant that analyzes LEED documentation, detects errors, and provides recommendations to improve certification readiness. Here's how it works:

  • Document Upload: Upload your LEED-related documents (PDF, DOCX, TXT)
  • AI Analysis: Our AI scans documents for compliance issues and opportunities
  • Recommendations: Get actionable suggestions to maximize LEED points
  • Interactive Chat: Ask questions about credits and get instant answers

Bonus: Reduce review time by 70% and increase certification success rates.

How accurate is Albedo?

Albedo is trained on official LEED v4 documentation and continuously improves. However, always verify AI suggestions with certified professionals.

What file types are supported?

Currently supports PDF, DOCX, and TXT files. More formats coming soon.
